FAQs

How long does gel polish last?

Typically 2 to 3 weeks with proper care, avoiding harsh chemicals and excessive water exposure.

Is dip powder safer than acrylic?

Dip powder is often considered gentler on the nails and has less odor, but proper removal is still crucial.

Can I book online or via chatbot?

Yes! Use our online portal or our chatbot to schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments 24/7.

What should I do if my nail chips?

Contact us right away. We offer complimentary fixes within 48 hours of your original service.

How often should I get a fill?

Every 2–3 weeks depending on your nail growth and lifestyle.
